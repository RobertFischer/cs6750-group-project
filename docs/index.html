Podcast Head-to-Head Discovery Prototype

March Madness style podcast discovery!

Caveats

This is a medium fidelity prototype and therefore is prototyping with the intent of answering specific questions. It is not a fully functional final product and therefore has many known limitations. You need to overlook these limitations in order to focus on the questions being asked by this prototype.

  • This prototype is testing the idea of using a head-to-head competition style. There is currently no smarts built into this system, but a final product would pick competitors based on previous responses from the users. Please do not consider the quality of the match-ups when evaluating the prototype, but instead the idea of having head-to-head matchups.
  • This prototype is giving a sense of the kinds of information that would be provided for your consideration. The actual layout and presentation of the information is very quick-and-dirty, but a final product would present that information in a more refined way. Please do not consider the presentation of the information, but instead the kinds of information being presented.
  • This prototype is demonstrating the interface, not actually providing a means for you to discover podcasts. The number of competitors is very small in this system, but a final product would be drawing from a variety of sources for podcast discovery. Please do not consider the particular podcasts, but instead the idea of a variety of podcasts eligible for competition.
